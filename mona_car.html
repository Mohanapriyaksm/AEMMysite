Tesla model S
The Tesla Model S is an all-electric five-door liftback sedan produced by Tesla, Inc., and was introduced in 2012. As of August 2020, the Model S Long Range Plus has an EPA range of 402 miles (647 km), higher than that of any other battery electric car.
